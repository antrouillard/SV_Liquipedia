===Format===
<section begin="Format"/>
====Group Stage====
* '''Group Stage:''' ''October 31st''
** 19 teams of 3 will be split into 2 groups of 12 teams each : 
*** Group A has the top seeded teams to allow them two chances of qualification to ''Finals''
**** {{bgcolortext|stayup|Top 7 teams}} of Group A will advance to the ''Finals''.
**** {{bgcolortext|staydown|Bottom 5 teams}} of Group A go fill Group B.
*** Group B has the lowest seeded teams.
**** {{bgcolortext|stayup|Top 5 teams}} of Group B will advance to the ''Finals''.
**** {{bgcolortext|staydown|Bottom 7 teams}} are eliminated.
** Each team will play 4 games in the ''Group Stage''.
** After the stage, 12 teams will advance to the ''Finals'' based off their scores in the ''Group Stage'':

* See ''[[SUPERVIVE/SNS/EU/November/1/Group_Stage|Group Stage]]'' tab for Group Draw, Standings, and Match Results.

====Finals====
* '''Finals:''' ''November 1st''
** The 12 remaining teams will play 6 games to compete for the first place in scores.
* See ''[[SUPERVIVE/SNS/EU/November/1/Finals|Finals]]'' tab for Match Results.
<section end="Format"/>
