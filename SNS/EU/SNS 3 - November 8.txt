===Format===
<section begin="Format"/>
====Group Stage====
* '''Group Stage:''' ''November 7th''
** 15 teams of 3 will be split into 2 groups : 
*** Group A has the top seeded teams to allow them two chances of qualification to ''Finals''
**** {{bgcolortext|stayup|Top 3 teams}} of Group A will advance to the ''Finals''.
**** {{bgcolortext|staydown|Bottom 9 teams}} of Group A go fill Group B.
*** Group B has the lowest seeded teams.
**** {{bgcolortext|stayup|Top 9 teams}} of Group B will advance to the ''Finals''.
**** {{bgcolortext|staydown|Bottom 3 teams}} are eliminated.
** Each team will play 4 games in the ''Group Stage''.
** After the stage, 12 teams will advance to the ''Finals'' based off their scores in the ''Group Stage'':

* See ''[[SUPERVIVE/SNS/EU/November/2/Group_Stage|Group Stage]]'' tab for Group Draw, Standings, and Match Results.

====Finals====
* '''Finals:''' ''November 8th''
** The 12 remaining teams will play 6 games to compete for the first place in scores.
* See ''[[SUPERVIVE/SNS/EU/November/2/Finals|Finals]]'' tab for Match Results.
<section end="Format"/>
